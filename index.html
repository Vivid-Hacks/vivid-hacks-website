What is Vivid Hacks?
Vivid Hacks is a 60 hour hackathon for everybody. We'll provide you with the mentors to bring your dream project to reality. Build something crazy. Something game-changing. Something cutting edge. Vivid Hacks is a weekend experience you won't want to miss. 😛
FAQs:
What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is an event where students come together to build and share something from scratch. You and a group of people will brainstorm an idea, and bring it to life during the event!
Who can join?
Vivid Hacks is open to everyone!
